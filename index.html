2. List all the document

  • db.employee.find({})

3. Querry for employees with salary greater than 30,000

  • db.employee.find({"salary" : {$gt : 30000}})

4. Querry for employees with experience more than 2 years

  • db.employee.find({"overallExp" : {$gt : 2}})

5. Querry for employees with graduation after 2015 and experience more than 1 year.

  • db.employee.find({"yearGrad" : {$gt : 2015}, "overallExp": {$gt : 1}})

6. Update the salary

  • db.employee.updateOne({"salary" : {$gt : 70000}}, {$set : {"salary":65000}})

7. Delete employes from Y company.

  • db.employee.deleteMany({"lastCompany": "y"})
